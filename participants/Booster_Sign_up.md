# WRH 30-Day Retention Booster Sign-up
*Beat the "Learn 20, Forget 80" pattern with spaced repetition.*

---

## **Why Sign Up?**
Research shows that most people forget 80% of what they learn in a classroom within 48 hours. The WRH program is designed to be different. By signing up for our **Retention Boosters**, you'll receive simple, low-pressure prompts to help you keep using the tools in your daily life.

### **What You'll Get:**
- **Day 1:** A quick reminder of the `Pattern Veto`.
- **Day 3:** A prompt to check your `Somatic Inventory`.
- **Day 7:** A "Mirror Moment" reflection question.
- **Day 30:** A 1-minute survey to see which tools are sticking.

---

## **How to Sign Up:**

### **Option 1: Digital Portal**
If you have access to the **WRH Web Portal**, log in and go to your **Profile Settings**. Toggle "Enable Retention Boosters" to **ON**.

### **Option 2: SMS/Text (Pilot Site A Only)**
If you are part of the **Stepping Stones Recovery** cohort, you can sign up via text:
1.  Text **WRH-PILOT** to **[Your Phone Number Placeholder]**.
2.  Reply with your **First Name** and **Cohort ID**.
3.  You will receive your first booster within 24 hours.

---

## **Privacy & Choice**
- **Voluntary:** You can opt-out at any time by texting **STOP**.
- **Confidential:** Your data is used only for your own retention and anonymized pilot metrics.
- **Non-Therapeutic:** These are educational prompts, not clinical therapy.
