## **[0:10 – 0:30] THE HOOK / CORE CONCEPT**
**Purpose:** Mechanism illustrated through the Architect.

**Core Concept:**  
This is a psychoeducational curriculum. We study the mechanisms of C-PTSD through one documented life — a man called the Architect. Not a therapy group. We learn the map.

The Architect was born three pounds, four ounces in 1982. He spent his first thirty days in a glass box. He had last rites at seven days old. By the time he was five, his home was a war zone. By the time he was forty, he had lost a brother, a marriage, custody of his children, and a decade to addiction. He is also still here — and he turned his nervous system into a curriculum.
