## **Non-Clinical Disclaimer**
The **What Really Happened (WRH) Trauma Psychoeducation Program** is strictly **psychoeducational and non-therapeutic**. 
- **Not Therapy:** WRH is not a substitute for clinical mental health treatment, psychotherapy, or medical advice.
- **Non-Exposure:** The program does not require participants to disclose or process personal trauma histories.
- **Educational Scope:** The curriculum focuses on the mechanics of the nervous system and the restoration of executive function through analytical instruction.
- **Liability:** Capitol Contracts LLC and its facilitators do not provide clinical diagnosis or treatment. All participants are encouraged to maintain their existing clinical relationships.

---

## **Safety & Warm-Handoff Protocols**
While WRH is non-clinical, we prioritize participant safety through:
- **Standardized Safety Protocols:** Yellow/Red Light protocols are integrated into every session.
- **Warm-Handoffs:** Facilitators are trained to route participants to licensed providers or the **Veterans Crisis Line (988, press 1)** if clinical needs emerge.
- **Voluntary Engagement:** Participants have full choice in their level of engagement and may opt-out at any time.
