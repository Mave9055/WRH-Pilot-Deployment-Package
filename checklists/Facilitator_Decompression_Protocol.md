# FACILITATOR DECOMPRESSION PROTOCOL
## **Systems Maintenance for High-Intensity Delivery**
**Version:** 1.0 (April 2026)  
**Tone:** Analytical, Systems-Focused (Kali Reaper)

---

## **1. THE RATIONALE**
Facilitating the WRH curriculum is a high-velocity systems operation. You are managing the nervous systems of 8–15 participants while maintaining your own regulation. Secondary trauma is not a "feeling" — it is a **systemic load** that requires a standardized reset protocol to prevent burnout and maintain fidelity.

---

## **2. IMMEDIATE POST-SESSION RESET (0–15 MIN)**
**Objective:** Disengage the "Kali Reaper" mask and ground the system.

1.  **Physical Exit:** Leave the room immediately after the last participant exits. Do not linger for "informal processing" unless it is a safety issue.
2.  **Somatic Discharge:** 
    *   **The Shake:** Shake out your hands and feet for 30 seconds to release physical tension.
    *   **The Exhale:** Three rounds of "Straw Breathing" (In for 4, out for 8 through pursed lips).
3.  **Environment Shift:** Change your physical environment. Step outside, change rooms, or wash your face with cold water.

---

## **3. THE DATA DOWNLOAD (15–30 MIN)**
**Objective:** Move the session load from your working memory to the record.

1.  **Fidelity Log:** Complete the session fidelity checklist. Note any "Yellow/Red Light" incidents.
2.  **The Mirror Check:** Did you stay in tone? Did you lose the room? Record the data without judgment.
3.  **The Hand-off:** If a participant hit a "Red Light," ensure the warm-handoff to clinical staff or 988 is documented and closed.

---

## **4. SYSTEMIC MAINTENANCE (24 HOURS)**
**Objective:** Prevent the "Hum" from building in the facilitator.

1.  **The Firewall:** Do not take participant stories home. You are a mechanic studying a machine, not a container for their pain.
2.  **Physical Regulation:** Prioritize high-quality sleep and physical movement within 24 hours of a session to process the cortisol load.
3.  **Peer Review:** If a session was particularly high-intensity, schedule a 10-minute "Systems Check" with a fellow facilitator or supervisor.

---

## **5. RED LIGHT INDICATORS FOR FACILITATORS**
If you notice the following, initiate a **Full System Reset**:
- Loss of "Kali Reaper" tone (becoming overly emotional or cynical).
- Avoidance of session preparation.
- Spiking "Hum" or sleep disruption following delivery days.
