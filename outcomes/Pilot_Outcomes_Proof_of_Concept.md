# WRH Pilot Outcomes & Proof-of-Concept
*Measuring the impact of trauma psychoeducation in real-world settings.*

---

## **The Problem: "Learn 20, Forget 80"**
Traditional psychoeducation programs often fail because participants only retain about 20% of the material. The WRH program is specifically designed to reverse this trend through **spaced repetition**, **active application**, and **low-arousal engagement**.

---

## **Pilot Site A: Stepping Stones Recovery of Arkansas**
*Status: Launching May–June 2026*

### **Target Metrics**
We are tracking the following key performance indicators (KPIs) for the Stepping Stones cohort:

| Metric | Target | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Cohort Retention** | **80%+** | Percentage of participants who complete at least 24 of 30 sessions. |
| **Tool Application** | **75%+** | Percentage of participants who report using at least one WRH tool (e.g., `Pattern Veto`) in the last 7 days. |
| **Symptom Awareness** | **90%+** | Percentage of participants who can correctly identify their "Hum" baseline. |
| **30-Day Retention** | **60%+** | Percentage of participants who still use at least one tool 30 days after the program ends. |
