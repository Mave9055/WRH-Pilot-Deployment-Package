# Pilot Site A: Stepping Stones Recovery of Arkansas
## Deployment Addendum — Version 1.1 (April 2026)

---

## **1. Site Context & Objective**
**Site:** Stepping Stones Recovery of Arkansas (Hartman, AR)  
**Setting:** Residential Recovery / SUD Treatment  
**Objective:** Execute a 30-session zero-cost pilot to establish proof-of-concept for the WRH curriculum in a residential recovery environment.

---

## **2. Zero-Cost Framing (2026 Launch)**
This pilot is structured as a **zero-cost mission-aligned investment** for the 2026 launch phase. Capitol Contracts LLC provides the full curriculum, facilitator training, and digital assets at no cost in exchange for:
- **High-Fidelity Delivery:** Commitment to the 30-session arc.
- **Outcome Data:** Anonymized retention and tool-application metrics.
- **Qualitative Feedback:** Testimonials and process improvement data for federal scaling.

---

## **3. Residential Recovery Integration**
- **Scheduling:** Sessions are integrated into the existing residential routine, ensuring no conflict with clinical or work-therapy requirements.
- **Peer Pods:** Participants are grouped into peer pods to reinforce tool application between sessions.
- **Voluntary Participation:** While integrated into the schedule, participation remains voluntary to maintain the "non-exposure" safety of the room.

---

## **4. Retention & Outcomes Tracking**
To combat the "learn 20, forget 80" challenge, this pilot utilizes:
- **Spaced Boosters:** Automated prompts at 1, 3, 7, and 30 days post-session.
- **20:80 Application Drills:** 80% of session time is dedicated to practical application of the 20% core content.
- **One-Tool Commitment:** Participants commit to using one specific tool (e.g., `The Pattern Veto`) between sessions.

---

## **5. Safety & Risk Management**
- **Yellow/Red Light Protocol:** Standardized room regulation as defined in the [Safety Rules](../checklists/Safety_and_Participation_Rules.md).
- **Warm-Handoff Protocol:** Immediate connection to the Veterans Crisis Line (988, press 1) or on-site clinical staff if a participant hits a "Red Light."
- **Non-Clinical Boundary:** Clear communication that WRH is psychoeducational, not therapeutic.

---
